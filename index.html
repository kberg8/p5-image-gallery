You had one job….!

“You Had One Job” is an expression used to call attention to perceived blunders made by individuals on the job. According to KnowYourMeme, a website dedicated to documenting Internet phenomena, the earliest known mention of the phrase took place in a scene from the 2001 remake of the heist film Ocean’s Eleven, in which Basher Tarr (a character played by Don Cheadle) scolds his team for missing a step that singlehandedly lead to the failure of an otherwise well-planned vault heist.View Gallery
